Misal Pav

Recipe Servings: 4

Prep Time: 15mins

Cook Time: 1hr 10mins

Total Cook time: 1hr 25mins

About Misal Pav Recipe:

A much-loved Maharashtrian curry that is a fiery melange of potatoes, freshly made spice paste and sprouted beans. Goes best with pav, it is usually served at the breakfast table but also makes for a light meal in itself.

Ingredients

For paste of gravy/sauce:

• 2 tbsp oil

• 1 tsp ginger paste

• 1 tsp garlic paste

• 1 cup onion

• 1 cup tomato

• ¾ cup coconut, grated

To make gravy/sauce:

• 3 tbsp oil

• Spice paste (as above),

• to taste salt

• 1 tsp red chilli paste

• 1 tsp garam masala

• ½ turmeric powder

• 1 tsp cumin-coriander powder

• ½ tsp cinnamon & clove powder

• 3 cups water (or as required to adjust the consistency)

To make usal:

• 3 tbsp oil

• 1 tsp garlic paste

• 1 tsp ginger paste

• ½ tsp hing (asafoetida)

• 1 + ½ cups steamed matki (moth) (soaked overnight)

• 1 cup potato (cut into square pieces), boiled

• ½ turmeric powder

• 1 tsp garam masala

• ½ tsp cinnamon-clove powder

• 1 lime juice

• 3 cups water (or as required)

• To taste salt

For garnishing

• onion, chopped

• Mix dry farsan

• Coriander, chopped

• To seve with: Paav, slice of lemon

How to Make Misal Pav

Prepare paste:

Heat oil in a pan. Add ginger paste, garlic paste and chopped onion. Saute till the onion is transculent. Now add chopped tomatoes and grated coconut. Mix well and saute it for few minutes. Allow the mixture to cool then grind it into a smooth paste.

Prepare gravy/sauce:

Heat oil in a pan. Add the ready paste into oil and saute it for about 2 minutes. Now add salt, red chilly powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, cumin-coriander powder, clove-cinnamon powder and mix it. Add water and cook till the mixture starts leaving oil. Transfer it in a bowl and keep it aside.

Prepare Usal:

Heat oil in a pan. Add ginger paste, garlic paste and asafoetida. Saute it for a minute and add soaked & boiled sprouts along with potatoes. Mix it well. Now add salt, turmeric powder, garam masala, clove-cinnamon powder and lime juice. Add water to it and cook it for about 8-10 minutes. Transfer it in a separate bowl.

Prepare Misal:

Take a serving bowl. First pour ready usal in the bowl and then pour the red gravy over it. Thereafter, add finely chopped onion and farsan to the mixture. Garnish it with chopped coriander. Serve it with pav and a slice of lemon.

Recipe Notes

You may also take mix sprouts to make usal.

The farsan can be substituted with Chivda as well.

Dum Paneer Kali Mirch

Recipe Servings: 4

Prep Time: 10mins

Cook Time: 45mins

Total Cook time: 55mins

Difficulty Level: Easy

About Dum Paneer Kali Mirch Recipe:

A fragrant curry brightened with black pepper. A favourite at dinner parties and lunch menu, this dish never to cease to satiate the taste buds and will leave you wanting more. Paneer cubes are cooked in flavourful spices in a dum or pressure, where steam can't release, making it a dish bursting with aromatic flavours. Serve with steamed rice or any Indian bread to have a wholesome meal. A delicious yet different dish to cook for a dinner party!

Ingredients

• 1 tsp oil

• 4 Cloves

• 4 Green cardamoms

• 1 inch cinnamon

• 1 Onion (fried till brown and blended with little water to make paste)

• Paste of 1 tsp ginger and 1 tsp garlic with 4 green chillies

• 3 tbsp dahi/ yogurt

• 1 tsp dhaniya/ coriander powder

• 1 tsp pepper powder

• ¾ cumin powder

• 1 tsp salt

• 2 tbsp cream

• ¼ tsp rang mirch/ paprika

• ¼ tsp haldi/ turmeric

• ¼ garam masala

• 250 gms paneer

• For garnishing coriander

• For garnishing fresh mint leaves

How to Make Dum Paneer Kali Mirch

1. Heat oil and add the cloves, cardamoms and cinnamon till it's nice and fragrant.

2. Now add onion paste and cook well.

3. Add ginger, garlic and green chilli paste. Saute.

4. Add yogurt / dahi.

5. Cook and add coriander powder, cumin powder, pepper powder, haldi and rang mirch / red chilli.

6. Add salt. Cook and add cottage cheese cubes and cream as well as 1/2 cup water.

7. Seal the pan with foil and put a lid on top.

8. Reduce flame to low and let it simmer for 15 minutes.

9. Now open it and cook further if you like to dry it up.

10. Check for salt adding more if needed, serve garnished with coriander and mint leaves.

Recipe Notes

Exclude the cream if you want to make it low fat.

Rice Dhokla

Recipe Servings: 5

Prep Time: 10mins

Cook Time: 30mins

Total Cook time: 40mins

Difficulty Level: Easy

About Rice Dhokla Recipe:

The all-time favorite Gujarati snack made with rice flour, suji and mild spices, dhokla is the perfect dish to njoy with your cup of tea. Light and lovely!

Ingredients

• 200gms rice flour

• 30gms suji (semolina)

• 100ml curd

• To taste sugar

• 4tsp oil

• ½ tsp mustard seeds

• 1-2 Whole red peppers

• A pinch of asafoetida powder

• Water (as per need)

• To taste salt

• 1 tsp lemon juice

How to Make Rice Dhokla

1. Mix together the rice flour, suji, yogurt, sugar and 1 tsp oil into a smooth paste.

2. Add enough water so as to make a batter of dropping consistency.

3. Add the lemon juice and asafoetida and leave to ferment overnight.

4. Keep the steamer over the stove for the water to boil.

5. Pour batter into prepared plates, cover and steam for 20 minutes. Remove, cool and cut into cubes.

6. Heat oil in a pan and add the mustard seeds, when crackling add the red pepper, when the colour darkens add 2 tbsp water, bring to a boil and pour over the dhokla cubes.

7. Garnish by sprinkling grated coconut and coriander leaves.
